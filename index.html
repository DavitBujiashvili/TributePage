Levan Andiashvili

The man who creates sounds !

Born in 1999 in Tbilisi, Levan Andiashvili is a sound-engineer, a DJ and a producer. He got interested in music from an early age and developed his own vision in which he blends sophisticated dark ambient with abstract textures and the most groove-filled realms of techno. Levan Andiashvili has appeared on the local scene since 2018 and has already performed at a number of venues including Khidi and Cafe-gallery.
